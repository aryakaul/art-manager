<b>Avatar the Last Airbender Book 3 Fire</b>
<b>Michael Dante DiMartino & Bryan Konietzko</b><br>
Hmmm. GOOD. Everything feels so satisfying and is 
wrapped up so well. It's very rare that a show can 
both initiate so many plots and character arcs AND 
resolve them all. Especially in 3 short seasons! 
Themes of redemption, hope, and love are interwoven 
quite nicely. A classic!
